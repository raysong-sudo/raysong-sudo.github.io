Pricing

Hands-on care, end to end.
Recovery, not maintenance.

Every visit is sixty minutes with one clinician. Manual therapy fills the majority of the session because it is what tissue, joints, and the nervous system physiologically respond to. The cost reflects the depth and continuity of the work, and most patients offset a meaningful portion through PPO reimbursement, HSA, or FSA.

Insurance

We accept select plans. And help you use yours.

We accept Medicare and select insurance plans directly. If you're unsure whether your plan is accepted, call before your first visit and we'll check with you.

For plans we're out-of-network with, partial reimbursement after the deductible is common for PPOs. We provide a superbill after every visit so you can submit directly to your plan.

Questions

  • What forms of payment do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards, HSA and FSA cards, and check. Payment is collected at the time of service.

  • How do I submit to insurance for reimbursement?

    We provide a superbill, a detailed receipt with all the codes your insurance needs, after every visit. You submit it directly to your plan. Most insurers process reimbursement within 30 to 60 days.

  • Can I use my FSA or HSA?

    Yes. Physical therapy is an eligible expense under both FSA and HSA plans. Use your card at checkout like any other payment.
