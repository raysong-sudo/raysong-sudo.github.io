For referring providers

Where your patients go when they need more.

Referral process

A phone call or a fax. We handle the rest.

Referrals can be sent by phone, fax, or patient self-referral with your name noted. We do not require a formal prescription for PT in New York State, patients can self-refer. However, if you'd like to send clinical notes, imaging reports, or operative summaries, we welcome them and incorporate them into the initial evaluation.

After the initial evaluation, we send a progress note to the referring provider summarizing our findings and proposed plan. Ongoing communication is available at any level of involvement, some providers prefer brief updates after each visit block; others prefer a summary at discharge. We follow your preference.
