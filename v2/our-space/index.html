The space · 16 W 32nd St, Suite 1007 · NoMad

A clinic built for one thing.

The clinic

Private rooms. No open gym floor. No waiting area full of other patients' treatments.

Suite 1007 at 16 West 32nd Street is a quiet floor in NoMad. The clinic has private treatment rooms, not curtained-off partitions, not cubicles. When you're in a session, no one else is in the room. The clinician works without interruption.

The space was built around the clinical model, not the other way around. Sixty-minute sessions require rooms that support sixty minutes of continuous, focused work. Manual therapy tables, ultrasound, e-stim, the tools are there. The distractions aren't.
