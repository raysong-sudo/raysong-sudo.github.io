Evidence-based, non-surgical, non-drug care, led by specialists. Whether you're dealing with scoliosis, pelvic pain, jaw tension, or chronic pain without a clear diagnosis, there's likely a program here designed for your situation.

What we treat

  • Schroth Method Three-dimensional scoliosis correction through breath, posture, and targeted movement. The non-surgical, evidence-based path to addressing the curve. Scoliosis
  • Pelvic floor Board-certified depth for pelvic pain, postpartum, and incontinence. The natural, non-surgical path to recovery. Pelvic pain, postpartum, incontinence
  • Acupuncture Real acupuncture by a licensed acupuncturist. Classical Eastern and Western, integrated with PT. Chronic tension, pain, nervous system
  • Manual therapy Hands-on as the first tool, every time. Thirty minutes of focused manual work per session. Pain, stiffness, injury recovery
  • Postural restoration Breath patterns and muscle asymmetry, the system that drives the posture, not the posture itself. Chronic pain, posture, breathing
  • Pain management Chronic and acute pain treated from the pattern, not the symptom. Whole-body, every session. Chronic & acute pain, fibromyalgia
  • TMJ care DDS-level jaw expertise in a PT setting. The jaw, the bite, the neck, all in one place. Jaw pain, headaches, clenching
  • Therapeutic Botox Clinical-grade Botox for jaw clenching, TMJ tension, and chronic migraine, used as an adjunct to the manual work. Anatomy-led. Aesthetic indications available on request. Jaw clenching, TMJ, migraine
  • Hypermobility Stability and load training for joints that move too much and hurt because of it. Joint instability, EDS, chronic pain
