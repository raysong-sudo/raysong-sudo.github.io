Programs

Care built around a goal, not a session count.

Four structured programs combining evidence-based physical therapy, acupuncture, and specialty care. Non-drug, non-surgical, and designed for people who want to address the whole picture, not just manage a symptom.

Most care is built around getting you out of pain and then stopping. These programs are designed to go further. They combine modalities because bodies don't work in isolation.

01

For chronic pain and tension

The Reset

1 evaluation + 3 PT sessions (manual therapy + modalities) + 2 acupuncture sessions. Six sessions total, structured over 6 weeks.

For people who've been managing something for months. Chronic tension in the neck, back, or hips. Pain that spikes under stress. The body that never fully recovered from something.

Physical therapy addresses the structural patterns. Acupuncture calms the nervous system that keeps the tension running. Modalities support the work between sessions.

Starting at $1,350 (includes evaluation)

For pelvic health

Pelvic Restore

1 evaluation + 4 pelvic floor sessions + 2 acupuncture sessions. Seven sessions over 8 weeks.

Postpartum recovery. Pelvic pain, pressure, leaking. Endometriosis. Anyone who wants to address pelvic health with the depth it deserves.

Pelvic floor addresses mechanics. Acupuncture addresses the nervous system component of pelvic tension and pain.

03

The most complete course of care

Full Spectrum

1 evaluation + 5 PT sessions + 3 acupuncture sessions. Nine sessions over 10 to 12 weeks.

For anyone who wants to address everything at once. Structural patterns, nervous system, recovery between sessions. People who've been putting this off and want to do it right.

PT handles structure and function. Acupuncture handles the nervous system and residual tension. Modalities reinforce the work between visits. All in one coherent plan.

04

Ongoing maintenance

Monthly Care

2 PT sessions + 2 acupuncture sessions per month. Arranged on a monthly basis, adjusted as needed.

For people who take their body seriously. High-performance professionals, athletes, parents who can't afford to be sidelined. Ongoing management of a chronic condition. Prevention over reaction.

Injury accumulates before it announces itself. Monthly care catches patterns before they become problems. PT keeps structure in check. Acupuncture keeps the nervous system regulated.
